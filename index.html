About Me


I'm Aimee, a passionate video editor with experience in creating videos using Final Cut Pro, Adobe Premiere Pro, and After Effects. It all started as a bored but creative arts kid, invested in the style and realness of the "2014" & "2016" YouTube vlogs era. That interest peaked—ironically—during the pandemic. From spending endless nights learning how to make YouTube intros on iMovie to now becoming a video editor who teaches others how to edit too! I enjoy the creative flow of every project I touch. This is a God-given passion, using my creativity to the fullest through a camera or phone lens. I’ll stop yapping now so you can dive into my portfolio :)))
